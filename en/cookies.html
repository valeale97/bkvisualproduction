Cookie Policy

This page is static and does not use tracking cookies. We use local storage only to remember your choice about the cookie banner.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help a website remember your preferences.

Cookies we use

  • bkvp_cookie_consent – value accepted after you accept the banner.
  • reCAPTCHA (Google) – always enabled to protect forms from spam. Google may set cookies for this purpose.
  • bkvp_prefs – optional; stores your preferences so the banner does not appear again.

Managing cookies

You can delete local storage for this domain in your browser settings, and the banner will appear again. Some features, like form protection, may rely on cookies or local storage.
